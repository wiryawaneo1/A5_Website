About Us

We are fully Sydney based, non profit organisation.

We provide clothing and other essential, free of charge, to the homeless and disadvantaged.

Content here

Our Mission

Provide good quality clothing and other essential items, free of charge, to the homeless and disadvantaged.

Our Vision

To help the 100,000 homeless people in Australia. Furthermore because of COVID-19, unemployment is on the rise and we are likely to see a dramatic increase, in the number of people sleeping in cars and living on the street.

GIVE

In this season, giving have such a huge impact on others. With the financial difficulties that people are facing, people around us need help more than ever.

Join us this season while we safely distribute essentials such as clothings but also masks to ensure the community's safety.

CARE

Caring for other's wellbeing is also what we try to achieve. With COVID-19, there area more essentials that everyone needs, more than just clothing and food.

In this season, we want to ensure everyone's wellbeing and safety. Thus, we want to engage with our community safely and also give them COVID-19 safety essentials that they need.

SUPPORT

The number of people being homeless is high and is rising. The current pandemic is note helping the situation either. Thus, we want to be able to help these people to get back on their feets.

With our movement, we want to do more than provide essentials, but also ensure that these people are able to get a second chance to turn things around for the better.
